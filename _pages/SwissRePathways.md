# Getting Involved with Research <br>

### West Lafayette, IN
1. “Purdue psychology participant registry”
2. [HHS Registry](https://hhs.purdue.edu/research-registry/participants/)

### Boston, MA
1. “Harvard University psychology participant registry”
2. [Study pool](https://studypool.psychology.fas.harvard.edu/participants)

### Other Areas
1. Find local Universities
2. Google "UNIVERSITY NAME psychology participant registry" or "UNIVERSITY NAME psychology research participant"
3. If neither give results, reach out directly to psychology department
   
### Other options
1. [Prolific](https://prolific.com)
2. [Mturk](https://mturk.com)
3. [Research Match](https://www.researchmatch.org/)
